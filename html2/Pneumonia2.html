What is Pneumonia?

Pneumonia is an infection that affects the lung tissues and causes the air sacs (alveoli) to fill with fluid or pus, leading to difficulty in breathing and persistent coughing.

Common Symptoms

  • Cough with or without phlegm.
  • Fever or chills.
  • Shortness of breath or difficulty breathing.
  • Chest pain that worsens with breathing or coughing.
  • General fatigue and loss of appetite.

Tips and Advice

  • Drink plenty of water and warm fluids to help expel mucus.
  • Get enough rest and sleep.
  • Avoid smoking or exposure to smoke.
  • Take your prescribed medications regularly as directed by your doctor.
  • Vaccinating children and the elderly against flu and pneumonia reduces the risk of infection.

When to See a Doctor Immediately

  • Severe shortness of breath or difficulty speaking.
  • Fever above 39°C (102°F) despite taking medication.
  • Bluish color of the lips or fingertips.

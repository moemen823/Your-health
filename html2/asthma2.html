Common Symptoms

  • Shortness of breath or difficulty breathing.
  • Wheezing or a whistling sound while breathing.
  • Frequent coughing, especially at night or early morning.
  • Chest tightness or a feeling of pressure.

Tips and Guidance

  • Avoid smoking and places with strong smells or dust.
  • Always carry your asthma inhaler and use it as needed or as prescribed by your doctor.
  • Practice deep breathing exercises regularly to improve lung function.
  • Visit your doctor regularly to monitor your condition and update your treatment plan.

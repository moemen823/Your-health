Common Symptoms

  • Chronic fatigue or unexplained tiredness.
  • Pain or heaviness in the upper right side of the abdomen.
  • Slight increase in liver enzymes during examination.
  • In advanced cases: yellowing of the skin or abdominal swelling.

Tips and Recommendations

  • Maintain a healthy weight and engage in regular physical activity.
  • Follow a balanced diet rich in vegetables and low in saturated fats.
  • Avoid alcohol and hydrogenated fats.
  • Regularly monitor liver function with your doctor.

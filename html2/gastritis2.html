Common Symptoms

  • Pain or burning sensation in the upper abdomen.
  • Nausea or urge to vomit.
  • Bloating or feeling full after eating small amounts.
  • Loss of appetite or sometimes weight loss.

Tips and Recommendations

  • Avoid spicy and fried foods as well as carbonated drinks.
  • Reduce coffee, tea, and acidic beverages.
  • Eat small, frequent meals instead of large ones.
  • Avoid smoking and manage stress as much as possible.
  • Consult your doctor if pain persists or vomiting blood occurs.

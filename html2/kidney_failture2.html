Common Symptoms

  • Swelling in the feet or face due to fluid retention.
  • General fatigue and body weakness.
  • Decreased urine output or change in color.
  • Nausea, loss of appetite, or metallic taste in the mouth.
  • Shortness of breath or difficulty concentrating.

Tips and Guidelines

  • Reduce salt intake and avoid processed foods.
  • Take medications only as directed by your doctor.
  • Drink enough water (unless otherwise advised by your doctor).
  • Avoid painkillers without medical consultation as they may affect the kidneys.
  • Regularly monitor kidney function and blood creatinine levels.

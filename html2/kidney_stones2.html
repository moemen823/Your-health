Common Symptoms

  • Severe pain in the lower back or one side of the body.
  • Pain that radiates to the lower abdomen or groin area.
  • Blood in the urine or urine that appears pink or brown.
  • Frequent urination or burning sensation while urinating.
  • Nausea or vomiting in some cases.

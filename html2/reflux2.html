Common Symptoms

  • A burning sensation or chest pain after eating.
  • Regurgitation of food or acid into the mouth.
  • Chronic cough or hoarseness of voice.
  • Difficulty swallowing or a feeling of a lump in the throat.

Tips and Advice

  • Avoid fatty, fried foods, and carbonated drinks.
  • Do not lie down immediately after eating; wait at least two hours.
  • Elevate the head of your bed slightly while sleeping to reduce reflux.
  • Lose weight if overweight and avoid smoking.
  • Consult your doctor if symptoms are frequent or severe.

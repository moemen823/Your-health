General Tips

  • Make sure to sleep 6 to 8 hours every day.
  • If you feel sick, visit your doctor for a check-up.
  • Wash your hands before eating to avoid germs.
  • Avoid staying up late — sleep after the Isha prayer and wake up for Fajr.
  • Do not spend more than one hour a day on your phone.
  • Stay close to God and keep a peaceful mind.
  • Don’t eat too many sweets.
